The signature pink hues of the dial actually stem from his favorite golf club, making this breathtaking design even more personal.</p>
<p>The RM38-02 features an entirely transparent crystal case that offers a 360-degree view of the beautiful movement within. The synthetic crystal middle case accentuates further the cleanliness and elegance of the watch and creates a float-in-light effect. The open dial shows a V-shaped bridge and a beautifully beautiful hand-painted pink piece of art, with the transparent case offering a beautiful background for the mechanical beauty of the watch to be showcased.</p>
