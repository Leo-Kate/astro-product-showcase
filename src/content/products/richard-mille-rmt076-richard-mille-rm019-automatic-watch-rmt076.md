<p>The Richard Mille RM019 is a luxury automatic watch that features a tonneau-shaped case made of 18k white gold and black sapphire crystal. The watch has a skeletonized dial that allows you to see the intricate movement of the automatic movement.</p>
<p>The RM019 has a power reserve of approximately 48 hours and features a date display at the 7 o’clock position. The watch is water-resistant to 50 meters and has a black rubber strap with a deployment clasp.</p>
<p>As with all Richard Mille watches, the RM019 is a high-end timepiece that combines exceptional craftsmanship and cutting-edge technology. It is a statement piece that is sure to turn heads and is a favorite among collectors and watch enthusiasts.</p>
