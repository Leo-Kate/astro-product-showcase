<p>Movement<br/>
The original model features a Breitling in-house movement; we use the most stable 7750 movement with a fish-scale pattern finish, and each movement is tested for 48 hours before leaving the factory.<br/>
Strap<br/>
Equipped with a brand-new, matching-color cowhide-rubber strap specifically designed for this series, featuring contrasting stitching for a highly stylish design. Extremely comfortable to wear. [Milanese steel strap] Original mold, complex craftsmanship. All details are identical to the original, and the strap can be removed with screws.<br/>
The new bezel design features stainless steel paired with an incredibly durable, scratch-resistant, and shock-resistant high-tech ceramic bezel ring. The metal ring surrounding the minute markers has been removed, allowing the bezel to perfectly integrate with the matching-color dial adorned with the classic brand logo.<br/>
Featuring a double-sided anti-reflective sapphire crystal, the addition of the panda chronograph sub-dials is undoubtedly the biggest highlight of this new product, making the new Breitling Superocean Heritage II Chronograph even more appealing.<br/>
Three contrasting sub-dials are located at the 3, 6, and 9 o’clock positions, corresponding to the 30-minute counter, 12-hour counter, and small seconds sub-dial, showcasing a classic mechanical aesthetic. The triangular hour hand, diamond-shaped minute hand, and the ends of the tapered hour markers feature Super-LumiNova® luminous material for easy time reading at night.<br/>
A date display window is also located between the four and five o’clock positions on the dial, adding a practical function to this classic dial.</p>
