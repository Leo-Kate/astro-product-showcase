<p>The RM007 is powered by the RM 005-S automatic movement, which is known for its precision and reliability. It has a power reserve of approximately 55 hours and features a date display at the 7 o’clock position.</p>
<p>One of the most unique features of the RM007 is its skeletonized dial, which allows you to see the intricate inner workings of the watch. The dial also features luminous hour markers and hands, which make it easy to read in low-light conditions.</p>
