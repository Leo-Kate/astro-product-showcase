<p>The Richard Mille RM018 Automatic Watch is a luxury timepiece that features a unique design and high-end materials. The watch has a tonneau-shaped case made of 18K white gold and measures 48mm x 39.70mm x 14.95mm. The case features a transparent sapphire crystal caseback, which allows you to see the intricate inner workings of the watch.</p>
<p>The RM018 is powered by an automatic movement that has a power reserve of approximately 55 hours. The movement features a variable-geometry rotor, which allows for efficient winding and optimal winding performance. The watch also features a free-sprung balance with variable inertia, which helps to ensure accurate timekeeping.</p>
<p>The dial of the RM018 is made of black onyx and features white Arabic numerals and hands. The dial also has a small seconds sub-dial at the 7 o’clock position. The watch is water-resistant to 50 meters and comes with a black rubber strap with a titanium buckle.</p>
