<p>The Richard Mille RM001 is a luxury automatic wristwatch designed and manufactured by the Swiss watchmaker Richard Mille. The watch features a unique and innovative design, with a tonneau-shaped case made of titanium and carbon fiber. The case measures 48mm x 39.7mm x 14.95mm and is water-resistant up to 50 meters.</p>
<p>The RM001 is powered by the Calibre RM005-S, an automatic movement with a power reserve of approximately 55 hours. The movement features a free-sprung balance with variable inertia, a double barrel system, and a rotor with variable geometry that allows for personalized winding.</p>
<p>The dial of the RM001 is skeletonized, allowing the wearer to see the intricate inner workings of the movement. The watch also features a sapphire crystal caseback, which provides a view of the movement from the back of the watch.</p>
<p>The Richard Mille RM001 is a highly sought-after timepiece among collectors and enthusiasts, with a price point that reflects its exclusivity and craftsmanship.</p>
