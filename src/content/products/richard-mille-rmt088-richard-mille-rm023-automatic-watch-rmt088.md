<p>The Richard Mille RM023 is a luxury automatic watch that features a tonneau-shaped case made of 18k rose gold or white gold, with a skeletonized dial that allows you to see the intricate inner workings of the watch. It has a power reserve of approximately 55 hours and is water-resistant up to 50 meters.</p>
<p>The RM023 also features a date window at the 7 o’clock position and a second time zone indicator at the 3 o’clock position, making it a great choice for frequent travelers. The watch is powered by the RM 023 automatic movement, which is made up of 32 jewels and beats at a frequency of 28,800 vibrations per hour.</p>
<p>As with all Richard Mille watches, the RM023 is known for its exceptional craftsmanship and attention to detail. It is a favorite among collectors and enthusiasts alike, and is often seen on the wrists of celebrities and athletes. However, due to its high price point and limited availability, it is considered a true luxury item.</p>
