<p>The Richard Mille RM055 is a masterpiece in both design and functionality. This Super Clone has a skeletonized black dial, which provides a window into the Swiss movement’s remarkable details. The case size is 49.90 x 42.70mm, which is a perfect size as not overly boldnto be garish, nor too small to be insignificant. The case is made from carbon fiber so it is extremely durable and surprisingly light weighing in at 74.3 grams. The titanium middle case is stronger than steel and its glossy sheen and natural texture perfectly matches the original. The strap is made of black rubber which is sporty, comfortable and perfect for everyday errands. This watch is stylish yet functional with a sapphire glass and a water resistance of 50 meters. The individual Swiss components are elegantly displayed through the clear glass back giving a perfect view of the beautifully finished movement.</p>
