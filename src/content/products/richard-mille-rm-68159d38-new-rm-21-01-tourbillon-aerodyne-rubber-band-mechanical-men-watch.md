<p>Brand: Richard Mille.RM 21-01.Tourbillon</p>
<p>Material: Manual tourbillon. Rubber band</p>
<p>Dimensions: 51x43x15mm</p>
<p>Description: One-to-one replica RICHARD MILLE Richard Mille’s new “RM 21-01 Tourbillon Aerodyne” aerodynamic tourbillon watch, showing inspiration from the aviation field. The new work still continues the brand’s iconic barrel-shaped design. The rose gold case is frosted on the front and brushed and polished on the side. It is matched with a TPT carbon fiber bezel and 8 columns made of carbon fiber. The unique pattern and texture contrast are pleasing to the eye. The bottom plate of the RM21-01 movement is undoubtedly the most eye-catching part of the entire watch</p>
