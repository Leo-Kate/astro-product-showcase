<p>The Richard Mille RM007 is a luxury automatic watch that features a tonneau-shaped case made from titanium or 18k rose gold, depending on the model. The case measures 45mm x 31mm and has a thickness of 11.25mm, making it a relatively large watch. The dial is available in a variety of colors and features large Arabic numerals, luminescent hands, and a date display at the 7 o’clock position.</p>
<p>The movement powering the RM007 is the Calibre RM 007, which is an automatic movement with a power reserve of approximately 55 hours. The movement is visible through the sapphire crystal caseback, which also allows for a view of the rotor decorated with the Richard Mille logo.</p>
<p>The RM007 is water-resistant up to 50 meters and comes with a rubber or leather strap, depending on the model. It is a highly sought-after watch among collectors due to its unique design, high-quality materials, and exceptional craftsmanship. However, it is also quite expensive, with prices ranging from around $50,000 to over $100,000 depending on the model and materials used.</p>
