<p>The new Rolex Greenwich GMT-Master Tiger’s Eye watch features a dial made of natural tiger’s eye stone, consistent with the materials used in the genuine product.<br/>
When light strikes at a 45-degree angle, the dial displays different three-dimensional effects, with a gradient effect in the tiger’s eye area! The classic Cerachrom ceramic bezel is paired with the innovative 3285 movement, accurately synchronizing two time zones. The two updated GMT watches are the all-rose gold “Root Beer” tiger’s eye and the all-yellow gold “Pepsi” tiger’s eye.</p>
<p>Regarding specifications, the watch has a 40mm case diameter and a thickness of approximately 13mm. The case and bracelet are made of 904L stainless steel. It uses high-definition sapphire crystal glass with a scratch resistance rating of 8 or higher. It features a 3285 integrated movement with a power reserve of 72 hours (approximately 67 hours in actual testing).</p>
