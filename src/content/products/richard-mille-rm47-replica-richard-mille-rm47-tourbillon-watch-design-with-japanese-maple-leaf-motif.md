<p>This pristine, faithful replica Richard Miller RM47 Tourbillon watch is a masterpiece that strives to blend with spirituality. Its design seeks inspiration in Japanese culture, showing modernity while paying deep respect to centuries-old traditions. Almost four years' work went into the creation of this watch, whereby it has compact movements that leave room for the wholly handmade set of samurai armor.</p>
<p>Its original design armor was sculpted with love by the artist Pierre-Alain Lozeron, and painted with passion by his wife Valérie; it represents different aspects of the ancestral culture from Japan. Made of 3N gold, the spirit of Bushido is what still runs throughout Japanese society.</p>
<p>The timepiece features a special Japanese maple leaf pattern that symbolizes the four seasons, elegance, beauty, and the fragility of life. Complementing the 50mm 3N gold case, black rubber straps give this timepiece a sporty, racing-style look. The watch is Swiss-driven, has a transparent skeleton design, and is water-resistant up to a depth of 50 meters.</p>
